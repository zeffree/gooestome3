Google Workspace Enterprise Standard vs Microsoft 365 E3

M365 E3 bundles productivity + a broad security and compliance stack — and gets meaningfully wider on July 1, 2026 when Defender for Office 365 P1 and three Intune Suite capabilities move into the base SKU at a new $42.50/user/month list price. Google Workspace Enterprise Standard covers productivity but leaves several E3-equivalent capabilities to be sourced from third parties. Pick best-of-breed alternatives from the Gartner Magic Quadrant for each gap and see the true TCO live.
